NASA- EVA Gloves

(Include NASA logo here, as well as some pictures).

This project was created for my Mobile and Ubiquitious Computing class. We got to present our work to NASA at their Wearable Technology Symposium.

For this project, I helped prototype a wearable sensor payload for astronaut gloves. Our prototype used an Arduino Teensy, some Force Sensing resistors and a custom sewn glove. To demonstrate our proof of concept, we used Processing to graph the output from the sensors on the glove. More info about our project can be found here.
